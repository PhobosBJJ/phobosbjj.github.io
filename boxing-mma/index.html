THE WORK

Three stages, one standard.

Every session builds on the last. Nobody is rushed into the next stage before the one before it holds up under pressure.

Fundamentals

Guard, footwork, distance management, the jab and basic combinations — corrected in real time, not left to be picked up by repetition alone. No prior experience required.

Sparring Readiness

Progressive pad work, defensive reads and controlled exchanges, building toward sparring only once the fundamentals and instincts are solid enough to make it useful rather than reckless.

Extending into MMA

Striking adapted for an MMA context, shootboxing, and wall wrestling — clinch and control work against the fence, taken directly from UFC-level training camps.

QUESTIONS

Before you get in touch.

Do I need any boxing experience to start?

No. Sessions start wherever you are — complete beginner, returning after time away, or already training and looking to have the fundamentals corrected properly.

Is this boxing coaching or MMA coaching?

Both, depending on what you're training for. Foundational sessions are pure boxing: footwork, guard, distance and timing. For those training toward MMA, striking is extended into shootboxing and wall wrestling — clinch work against the fence.

Will I be made to spar before I'm ready?

No. Sparring is introduced progressively, once fundamentals and defensive habits are solid. Pacing is set to the individual, not a fixed programme length.

Where do sessions take place?

Combat Sports Centre, Vulcan Road, Solihull, B91 2JY.

How is this different to a group boxercise class?

Sessions are private, one to one or small group by arrangement, with technique corrected in real time — not a fixed class routine run for a room.
